Privacy Policy

Last updated: 8 July 2026

The short version

WizardsTome is built to be private by default. It has no user accounts, no advertising, and no analytics or tracking. We do not run a server that stores your data, and we do not collect, sell, or share any personal information. Your books, reading progress, bookmarks, highlights, notes, and settings all live only on your device.

The files you open

You add your own files through your device's standard file picker or the system share sheet. The app reads only the files you explicitly choose. These files, and everything you create from them (progress, bookmarks, highlights, notes), stay on your device and are never uploaded anywhere by us.

Information we collect

None. WizardsTome does not collect or transmit any personal or usage data to us. We have no analytics SDK, no crash-reporting service that identifies you, and no advertising.

Optional features that use the internet

There are exactly two features that make a network request, and only when you actively trigger them. In both cases we do not operate a server, and we do not store or log the request — it goes directly from your device to a third-party service.

  • Online dictionary fallback. The app ships with an offline dictionary. If you look up a word that isn't in it, the single word is sent to the free Free Dictionary API (dictionaryapi.dev) to fetch a definition. Only that word is sent — nothing about you or your library.
  • AI cover generation. If you tap "Generate art cover," the book's title is sent to the free image service Pollinations.ai to create a cover image. Only the title is sent.

These are the only times any information leaves your device. Each third-party service processes your request under its own privacy policy. If you never use these features, WizardsTome makes no network requests at all.

Permissions

WizardsTome does not request broad storage access. It reads files through the Android document picker and the share sheet only. It does not access your location, contacts, camera, microphone, or photos beyond the specific images you choose to import.

Children's privacy

WizardsTome does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. Because we collect no data at all, there is nothing to collect from a child either.

Data retention & deletion

Because your data lives on your device, you are in full control. Uninstalling the app, or clearing its storage in your device settings, permanently removes everything the app holds. We hold nothing to delete on our end.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update it on this page and revise the date above. Material changes will be reflected here before they take effect.

Contact
